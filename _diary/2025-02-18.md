
### MAC Address
* Media Access Control (MAC)
	* 6 Octet(48 bit)
	* Organizational Unique Identifier(OUI) + Host Identifier(Serial)
* Address Resolution Protocol (ARP)
	* `Broadcast [src_ip,src_mac,dst_ip,(dst_mac???)]`
	* Host or Router
### Xxxcast
* Unicast
	* `[src_mac,dst_mac]`
	* drop/processing
* Broadcast
	* To all nodes (broadcast domain)
